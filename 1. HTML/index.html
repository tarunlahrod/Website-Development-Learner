What's the agenda
behind the conference

We are here to share the vision.

The motivation behind organising InnoviCon 2019 is to bring together top researchers and industry experts in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning and make their understanding of the recent trends accessible to undergraduates and professionals who are commencing their exploration of the field.

The conference will have multiple tracks with varied focal points so that each attendee can make the most of wealth of information and technical know-how being offered. Over the course of two days, the attendees can choose to attend any of the keynotes, panel discussions and workshops being held.

InnoviCon will be a great place to network with fellow developers and researchers who strive to build their future in AI related fields. We hope to create a community of motivated students and professionals who believe in sharing the knowledge they have acquired in their work to achieve innovative solutions and improved results.

Theme

Artificial Intelligence

With technology moving at breakneck speed, we are now in the midst of a surge of new breakthroughs, where we carry more power in our pockets today. One such promising effort is ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE. Artificial Intelligence is a way of making a computer think rationally as humans do. This type of “deep learning” allows machines to process information for themselves on a very sophisticated level, allowing them to perform complex functions. It involves programming the computers for certain traits such as knowledge, reasoning, problem solving, perception, learning, planning, ability to manipulate and move objects. From self driven cars to facial recognition technology, the key advances in AI have emboldened the public, policymakers and investors into believing that human-level machine intelligence is just around the corner.

Exploiting the potential of this technology further, InnoviCon 2019 is a platform to delve deep into the emerging AI techniques and technologies.It brings together technology leaders and professionals to explore how AI systems are transforming operations, rewriting business strategy and creating new engines for rapid and disruptive growth. From presentations to discussions to latest technology insights, you will experience the latest in AI at this uniquely conceptualised conference.

Aravind is passionate about evangelising technology, meeting developers and helping in solving their problems. He is a backend developer and has seven years of development experience.

Previously, He worked at McAfee Antivirus as a Sr. Software Engineer in Cloud Security Domain. He has deep interest in Search, Machine Learning, Security Incident Analysis and IoT tech. Keynote speaker 02

Ankit Prasad

At the age of 24, Ankit Prasad founded Bobble Keyboard and leads the team as the CEO, backed by 16 reputed angel investors, and SAIF Partners. He's in the Forbes 30 under 30 Asia list and is an IIT Delhi Dropout.

He has also been the founder-CEO of the famous platform Touchtalent. His team works on bleeding edge technology to make mobile conversations expressive and personal. Bobble has over 15 million users in less than three years of launch.
